My School

The name of my school is Handsworth Grange Community Sports College

It is at Handsworth Road and has over 1,000 students enrolled.


It has a large sports hall and a library.


It has a large canteen and a large playground.


It has a large field and a large gym.


There are many different subjects to study.


My favourite subject is English.

My second favourite subject is Design and Technology.


My least favourite subject is PSHE.


Overall, my school is a really great place to learn.
